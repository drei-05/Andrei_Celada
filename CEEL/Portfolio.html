Skills:

Figma (Wireframing, Prototyping)

I use Figma to build the blueprints of digital designs. This means I can plan user-friendly layouts (wireframing) and create interactive models (prototyping) to test and perfect a design's flow and functionality before the final artwork is even created.

Canva (Quick Graphics, Social Media)

I leverage Canva to produce high-impact graphics efficiently. This skill is perfect for creating a strong and consistent visual presence across social media platforms and for crafting compelling marketing materials with speed and precision.

Creative Writing

I combine strong visual design with compelling creative writing. This means I can craft clear project concepts, articulate design rationales, and write engaging copy that enhances the overall story and effectiveness of a brand's identity.

Ibis Paint X (Digital Illustration)

I utilize Ibis Paint X for handcrafted digital illustrations. This allows me to add unique, artistic flair and custom-drawn elements to my projects, giving them an original and personal touch that stock imagery can't provide.
